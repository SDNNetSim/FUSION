Engine
=======

The Engine orchestrates the simulation process, serving as the central component that connects and controls every
aspect of the simulation. From managing the SDN controller to coordinating routing, spectrum assignment,
and statistical analysis, the Engine ensures seamless integration and interaction between different modules,
facilitating comprehensive simulation runs and insightful analysis of network behavior.
